WHO WE ARE

We are a natural farm using local manure and cover crops instead of chemical fertilizers. We harness natural predators with planting diversity avoiding harmful sprays.

Once you realize how toxic supermarket food is, you will buy naturally farmed food and begin to heal.

WHAT IS A NATURAL FARM?

The 4 Rules of Natural Farming:

1 - No cultivation. No plowing or turning of soil.
2 - No chemical fertilizer or prepared compost.
3 - No weeding by tillage or herbicides.
4 - No dependence on chemicals.

(Masanobu Fukuoka "One Straw Revolution")
